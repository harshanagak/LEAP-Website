League for Environmental Awareness and Protection (LEAP) is a student club in NIT-Trichy aiming to disseminate the urgent issues prevailing in the environment and in the campus among the students of NIT-Trichy. The club was founded to create a new approach to spread awareness and educate the students so that they develop a high level of competence in global environmental issues and to labor themselves in finding suitable solutions at the college level itself.

Accomplishments


Biggest FEAT in association with NSS (IBR EVENT) in mid 2019

We volunteered to organize and teach in a mega drive event to bring about awareness amongst the masses on the issue of Plastic Waste Management. Our members traveled to over 45 schools in and around Trichy to carry out the event and spread awareness to 28000+ students in and around Trichy, and this has been recognized in The India Book of Records. All of the volunteers received a medal from the Deputy Collector of Trichy as a token for our efforts. The massive event got registered on INDIAN BOOKS OF RECORD.
